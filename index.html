Welcome to the misc-dev wiki!

This repository contains miscellaneous classical texts encoded in TEI P5 or EpiDoc formats. The files are encoded by the Open Greek and Latin research team (researchers and student assistants). While adequate, more can and should be done to improve the XML (the -dev(elopment) suffix indicates the repository is open to improvement).
